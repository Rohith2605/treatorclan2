TREATOR CLAN

  • TREATOR CLAN is basically bringing up all the Treator across the world under single platform. They can be doctors, surgeons, nursing staff etc.
  • Not only Treators ,but also the normal citizens can create their profiles and have access to all the treators in the world.
  • The people can view their profile that can consist of their workplace details, experience, Insights, success stories.
  • The citizens can create their profile as Family Head/ Family member.
  • The Family Head can monitor the health profiles of the family members from newly born infants to elderly people.
    Based on their created profiles and activity in the Treator Clan, they are given with a weekly report and health index is generated on a scale of 1 to 10
